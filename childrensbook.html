"I Love You This Much!" is a children's book describing how 'big' a mothers love can be.

Book 1

Book 2

The illustrations are a mix of traditional and digital media, featuring colorful, playful, and imaginative scenes that bring the characters and environments to life. The art style is reminiscent of classic children's books, with bold outlines, expressive characters, and intricate details that are inviting to young readers.

16 pages of full-color illustrations, Whimsical typography and font choices, Custom-designed characters and environments, Incorporation of textures and patterns to add depth and visual interest, Innovative page layouts that encourage interactive storytelling.

This book is designed for children aged 0-5, with the aim of putting them to sleep. The story is simple enough for early readers to follow, while the illustrations provide a rich visual experience that will delight children of all ages.
