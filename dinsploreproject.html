Dinosplore
2023
This project was a submission for university in my second year. I was instructed to create a web outcome to engage children with their studies.

The website includes 3 Games and a quiz. Orignal logo and characters made by me. Animation included in the site. Bright colours and creative icons to add visual interest. Interesting subject matter and age appropriate content for its target users.

The game was designed for young children, ages 5-6. With this being target demographic, the website is easy to use and includes design features and learning content that is appropriate for this audience.
Logo & Characters
Technical Details: HTML, CSS & JavaScript. Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign) Digital Graphics and illustration techniques.
