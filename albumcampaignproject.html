Album Campaign
2021
Project Planning
This project was a submission in first year for my graphic design component, where I was instructed to craft an Album Creation and Marketing Campaign for an artist of our choice.

A Vinyl cover: Back, Front & Sleeve. Several styles of posters, with alternate compositions. Self-Designed Typography. Incorporation of digital appliques and patterns to add depth and visual interest. Merchandise reflective of the product & style.

My mission was to create a fun cover for a hypothetical album campaign for my chosen musical artist, Harry Styles. Taking the context into consideration, that being his quirky style and young female fan-base, I had to create media that would match his image and appease his audience.
Creative Process
My project, as always, began with thorough research into both the context and the client. Since this was a branding initiative for an artist, it was crucial to truly capture their essence. Given the artist's distinctive and unique style, I was able to weave many of these elements into the overall design.

However, I also sought to infuse my own stylistic choices into the work. I incorporated a wealth of original illustrations and meticulously planned each design by hand—my preferred approach, which grants me greater creative freedom.

To clarify the project's direction, I developed several mood boards that visually represented my ideas. Keeping the client informed throughout the design process was essential, making this step a vital part of our collaboration. The overarching theme was to create something quirky that resonated with the Harry Styles brand while remaining palatable for the audience. It felt necessary to play on the theme of 'watermelons,' as it added a playful touch that harmonized with the artist's style.
